ASISTENTE CROHN (TFG)

Introduction

This application was born as my Bachelor Thesis at the University of Santiago de Compostela (USC, Spain) as an Android application to improve Crohn´s disease patients´ quality of life.

It was completely my own idea, previously designed in Adobe XD (it was my first approach with this tool) and developed in Android Studio with Java 13 and XML.

You can check it out at Play Store. It has had an impact on press and RRSS due to its benefits.
